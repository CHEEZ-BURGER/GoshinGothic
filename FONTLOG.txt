FONTLOG for GoshinGothic

This file provides information on the changes made to this font software.

Modified font:
- GoshinGothic

Source fonts:
- Pretendard
- Noto Sans Korean

Contributor:
- The Korea University Weekly

Detailed changes:
- Most glyphs and base font data were taken from Pretendard.
- Hanja glyphs were imported from Noto Sans Korean.
- Quotation mark, comma, and inequality sign glyphs were newly designed for this modified version.
- The font was renamed for redistribution as a modified version.
- Font metadata and copyright information were updated for release.
